ABOUT ESHE

At ESHE, LLC the goal is to inspire each individual to live their own truth. Through a holistic approach specially designed for ESHE clients known as THE WAE (wellness, alignment, ease), clients learn to trust themselves in order to make more whole and healthy decisions.​ Eshe is a swahili word meaning "life".

  • Group Wellness Life Coaching
  • Individual and Group Vision Boarding
  • Mindfulness Classes
  • Parenting Consulting
  • Homeschooling/Unschooling Consulting

ORIANA LEE

Coach/Consultant, Educator & Spiritual Advisor

Oriana Lee has partnered with hundreds of individuals and famililes to help them learn to live the life they want, both personally and professionally. Oriana has the special gift of helping people discover who they are, what they want, what's blocking them, and what works to move them forward. The goal is to help clients come up with their own solutions and helping to empower them to achieve successful results. Much of Oriana's experience has been within the entertainment industry, and with women. Oriana has also spent a great deal of time working in churches, spiritual retreats, jails, shelters, and inner-city communities. While trained as both a minister and counselor, Oriana Lee prefers work as a coach, as it allows for the focus to not be on the problem, but on the solution.
